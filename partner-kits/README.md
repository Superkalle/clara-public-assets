# [DRAFT] Public Assets — Partner Kits

# Purpose

Externally shareable enablement bundles for partners and multipliers operating under explicit CLARA positioning alignment.

A partner kit contains:
- approved executive onepager
- approved boardroom deck
- approved whitepaper(s)
- partner-safe messaging guide
- external sharing rules

Partner kits are not internal sales kits.

They are public-facing distribution bundles.
