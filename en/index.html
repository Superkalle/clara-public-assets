Why this hub exists

Organizations are becoming faster through AI, but they are not automatically becoming clearer. Stakeholder coordination grows. Assumptions remain implicit. Governance visibility decreases. Decision quality becomes unstable under pressure.

The assets published here exist to support executive conversations about this gap — and the structured governance layer CLARA introduces to close it.

How to read these assets

Every asset reinforces the same positioning: CLARA as a Decision Governance Platform, not an AI assistant or productivity tool. Differences between assets are differences of audience and depth — not of message.

Use the onepager for first contact. Use the boardroom deck for executive briefings. Use the whitepapers for strategic context. Use the partner kits for aligned external distribution.
